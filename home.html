About Us

Welcome to Climate Corner! We are an interactive website with a goal of promoting awareness about climate change & encouraging everyone to take action to help protect our planet. That being said, we believe taking on these actions should be fun! Below, you can create a checklist of actions to better the environment, & you can check them off as you complete each action. Look at our list of Eco-Level challenges for things you can do & add to your checklist. Each time you complete a challenge, you can gain the corresponding number of points & move up an Eco-Level. But don't worry, you won't be alone on this adventure! You can find & make new friends with other users that care about the environment & compete with each other. You can also find articles below & stay up to date on current world events pertaining to environmental problems, solutions, & going-ons.
